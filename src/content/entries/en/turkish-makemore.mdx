The model above is real: a tiny character-level MLP (Karpathy's makemore part 2)
trained from scratch on ~560 Turkish first names. It looks at the previous three
characters, embeds them, runs one tanh hidden layer, and samples the next
character until the name ends.

It's 4,234 parameters, small enough to ship as a 90 KB JSON file and run in a few
lines of plain JavaScript. Press generate and the sampling loop runs on your
machine, no server. The training code, weights and demo live in [their own
repo](https://github.com/demirbasaf/turkish-makemore); this page just loads that
artifact.
